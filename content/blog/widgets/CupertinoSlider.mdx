---
title: CupertinoSlider
---

An iOS-style slider.

### Example

```dart liveslice=CupertinoSlider
CupertinoSlider(
  value: _progress,
  min: 0.0,
  max: 100.0,
  onChanged: (value){
    setState(() {
      _progress = value.roundToDouble();
    });
  },
)
```
